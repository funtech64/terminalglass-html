Infrastructure

Hosting and Infrastructure Costs

Terminal.Glass software and deployment service are one part of the total cost. Server or cloud infrastructure may be billed separately by your provider — or covered by hardware you already own.

You may use your own compatible Linux server, on-premise hardware, a private datacenter host, or a customer-owned cloud account. Managed hosting can be selected where available.

Final model capability depends on the server resources you choose. Larger models and GPU workloads require appropriately sized hosts. We provide a hosting size recommendation with every order.

Some deployments use cloud-accelerated model inference with a small private Linux host. Those workflows are not fully offline — prompts processed by Ollama Cloud leave your network per Ollama's terms.
